Let's look at something also very useful. You might know some friends who can play by ear and play any 

song just by listening to it. Well in this case, this gift is not given to everyone but it is important 

to discover whether or not you have it. Of Course this can be trained but that comes with experience. 

If you would like to skip this chapter then you may. 

Lets start by understanding this first. From before we know that sheet music consists of pitch and 

rhythm. We will be focusing on pitch for now. Ever heard of the phrase pitch perfect? Well it comes 

from when someone can determine what note any sound is kind of like when you see colors. Someone can 

identify music into notes like you may identify a picture into colors. So if I were to say something they 

may say something like I said it in A flat or A sharp. But those things are not important. Attached is a 

clip which shows how every note on a piano sounds. Lets try to listen and understand to them!
